Hi, I am an incoming master student at Carnegie Mellon University. I received my undergraduate degree from Dalian Maritime University, Dalian, China. My interests include Deep Learning and Computer Vision. Currently, I work on visual explanation and adversarial attack, supervised by Prof. Xia (Ben) Hu and have a close cooperation with Fan Yang and Mengnan Du.

During my undergraduate, I worked at Everspry as a development intern supervised by Junjian Cui from Apr 2019 to May 2019, and worked at Horizon Robotics as a research intern supervised by Zhenghua Chen from July 2018 to Jan 2019. I also worked at Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences supervised by Prof. Jie Yu in 2018 Spring. I exchanged at UC Berkeley in 2017 Fall, and worked at RISE Lab as undergraduate research assistant on Clipper group.
